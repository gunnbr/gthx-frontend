Commands Interacting with GThx
To set a factoid, address gthx directly and type the name of a factoid, the keywords "is" or "are", and the value to set. If the command works correctly, gthx replies with "Okay."
gthx: filament is meltable plastic used as raw material to your printer. you: Okay.
To get a factoid, just type the name of a factoid with a question mark or exclaimation point at the end:
filament? filament is meltable plastic used as raw material to your printer.
Add to a factoid by using the 'also' keyword when setting a factoid:
cake? cake is delicious gthx: cake is also a lie you: Okay. cake? cake is delicious and is also a lie
To delete a factoid, use the forget command. Gthx then replies to let you know whether or not it was successful:
forget filament you: I've forgotten about filament
Factoids that start with a <reply> tag cause gthx to reply without using the name of the factoid:
gthx: pics is <reply>Pics or it didn't happen! you: Okay. pics? Pics or it didn't happen!
The tag !who in a factoid is replaced with the nick of the person who triggered the factoid query:
gthx: hello is <reply>Hello, !who! you: Okay. hello? Hello, you!
To save a message for a user the next time they're seen online, directly address gthx and use the tell command:
gthx: tell gunnbr I finished that design for you. you: I'll pass that on when gunnbr is around.
Retrieving a message happens automatically the next time a message with a waiting message says something:
Hello, everyone. you: 2 hours, 15 minutes, 50 seconds ago <gunnbr> tell you I got the webpage updated.
Find when a user last spoke by using the 'seen' command:
seen gunnbr? gunnbr was last seen in #reprap 1 hour, 13 minutes, and 12 seconds ago saying 'I have to fix some things on the website.'.
An asterisk can be used to match partial nicks:
seen gunn* gunnbr was last seen in #reprap 1 hour, 13 minutes, 12 seconds ago saying 'I have to fix some things on the website.'. gunner was last seen in #reprap 3 days, 4 hours, 36 minutes, 21 seconds ago saying 'How can I fix my printer?'. gunnbr_ was last seen in #reprap 207 days, 8 hours, 45 minutes, 31 seconds ago saying 'Ugh. The website crashed again.'.
